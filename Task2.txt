Answer the questions:
Q1. Given bracket sequence: [((())()(())]]. Can this sequence be considered correct? 

A1. No, this is can't be considered correct.

Q2. If the answer to the previous question is “no”, 
then what needs to be changed in it to make it correct? 

A2. The brackets should be balanced. There's many ways for it:
1) Delete the first '(' and the last ']'. [(())()(())]
2) Replace Pre-last ']' to ')'. [((())()(()))]
3) In case, if it is acceptable. Replace the 1st '(' into '['. [[(())()(())]]
